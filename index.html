About Me

I am a PhD researcher in QUVA Deep Vision Lab, under supervision of Professor Arnold Smeulders. I received my bachelor degree (BSc) in Electrical and Electronics Engineering in 2013 from Middle East Technical University (METU), Turkey and my master degree (MSc) in Signal Processing from Tampere University of Technology (TUT), Finland in 2015. My master's thesis, which is on Unsupervised Anomaly Detection in unstructured system logs, was supervised by Professor Moncef Gabbouj.
